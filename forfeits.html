Forfeits

A collection of some popular and creative forfeits that you can use as part of any drinking game.

Contact us by clicking the feedback button at the bottom of this page for your chance to make an addition to this list.

Browse the list

Select one of the dropdowns below to view their definition

All Fours
The loser must only travel on all fours for a limited amount of time.
Beer Bong
Neck a full drink using a beer bong funnel and pipe.
Cheese On Crackers
Shave a thin slice of soap off of the top of a bar of soap. Place it on top of a cracker or similar carbohydrate and eat.
Chug Cup
Everyone involved puts a little bit of their own drink into a glass until full, the forfeit is to drink it whole. This is usually done before a game so people are more reserved with what they pour in and so it creates more dread about having to do the forfeit.
Dancing Monkey
Whether it is for half an hour or for the entire evening, the person facing the forfeit is now the official dancing monkey, strutting their stuff anytime someone demands.
Edward Ciderhands
Tape a bottle of cider to each hand, only when the person finishes the drink can they take them off. Top tip: Some cider bottles go up to 4 litres.
Flirt
The person must flirt with a person chosen by another individual or if allowed, a person of the losers' choice
Flour Power
Eat a spoon full of flour, nothing more
Ice-T
Soak a t-shirt in water, ring it out a little, place it in the freezer for 30 mins or until frozen. The forfeit is then to wear the top as normal until it thaws out.
Dilemma
Provide three different consumable options for the person which includes at least one alcoholic option, creativity can be used for the others. The person must choose one to consume as the forfeit, so try to make their decision a dilemma.
Graffiti
A person is drawn on by another individual on any body part, private or public.
Outcast
For a limited amount of time, the person is to be ignored by everyone. This includes touching speaking or looking into their eyes.
Shit Shave
The person who is facing the forfeit is to be shaven by another person on their face, legs, head or elsewhere.
Shot Roulette
Multiple shots are poured and the person would have to be either blindfolded to choose one or use a purpose built roulette wheel which chooses for them.
Sock It
This forfeit involves consuming a drink through a sock by placing it over the rim of the glass, a decision needs to be made about whos sock it is.
The Salon
One of the longer lasting punishments, the person facing the forfeit must be fake tanned on 1 particular part of the body.
Toilet Troll
The task is that the person must enter the bathroom looking suspicious and offer 'a hand' to others in the bathroom without actually using the facilities themselves.
TP
Take one square off of a roll of toilet paper and eat whole. Depending on whether the paper is scented or multi-ply, some efforts may be trickier than others.

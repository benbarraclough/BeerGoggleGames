Shot Recipe List

A collection of shot recipes of different tastes and styles, appealing to all palates.

Shots can be fun and interesting but when mixed wrong can be the end of somebodys night. Take some inspiration from what we have listed below, make you own and get creative.

Our simple recipes show you how they are created, the measurements you need and how they're drank.

About Us

We're a small non for profit website that offers info on all things drinking with our main focus being drinking games.

We hope to offer a fun and easy way to get drunk with friends as well as people you don't know. Our goal is to make sure that no night is the same and to provide entertainment for parties, crappy nights out, to host great parties and small social events.

Being a non for profit business we rely on donations alone and appreciate any financial help towards the development of the site using the PayPal donate button located below and at the bottom of every page.

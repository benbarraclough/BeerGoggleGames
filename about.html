About Us

We are a small non for profit website that offers information on all things drinking with our main enthusiasm being drinking games. Our site offers a wide range of games under various different categories so that you can get creative with your drinking and ensure that no night is the same.

The service we provide hopes to offer a fun and easy way to get drunk with friends and people you don't know without being awkward or playing the same games over and over again. Use our site to entertain yourselves on crappy nights out, to host great parties, to liven up social events and making video chats interesting.

We hope to be able to deliver all sorts of new and exciting drinking ideas such as fun products, event ideas and other activities but we need help to do it. Being a non for profit business we rely on donations alone and would greatly appreciate any help using the paypal button at the bottom of the page, thank you

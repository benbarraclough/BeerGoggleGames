Double Down

  1. Home
  2. Coin Games
  3. Double Down

Description

Bet wisely when its your turn, take a drink or double down to risk it all

Equipment

  • 1 centre cup
  • 1 Coin
  • 1 drink each

Setup

Divide the shot glasses between 2 teams and construct your side of the board using a pizza box (or multiple pieces of cardboard) and a pen to draw both the opponents 6x6 grid and your 6x6 grid. Then place all of your shots on a grid square of your choosing marked with an "S" so they can't be changed. Follow this short setup video by tipsy elves for assistance.

Aim of the Game

Try and drink the least

How to Play

Each player gets 6 cards which they keep to themselves. Every round a player must play one of their cards (the higher the better). Once everyone has chosen a card to play, these are all flipped over at once.

If you have a higher card than someone else, you are safe

If you have the lowest card, then you must add 2 fingers of drink to the centre cup & flip the coin after calling the side you think its going to land on.

If you guess the coin flip inorrectly, you can either drink it or double down and add two more fingers to the centre cup before playing the double down round

In the double down round, another player must spin the coin for you on the table and you must stop it whilst its spinning with one finger so it stands up vertically.

If you guess the coin flip correctly or successfully pass the double down round, you move to the next round with no drinking

You then keep playing your cards until you have none left and then pick up another six, to repeat the game

Rules

  • Peaking is disciplined by having one of your shots taken away
  • Reverse psychology and insults are encouraged
  • No takebacks (once you said it, consider it guessed)
  • Mark the spots where you place your shots with an "S" so theres no cheating

Burn The Napkin

A game requiring fire safety and a precise lighter skills. Players can light the napkin as much as they wish but must not let the coin that is sitting on top fall through.

Doom

Play your cards wisely to avoid the big centre chug cup of doom. If not, hope that chance is in your favour allowing you to escape... otherwise you drink.

Double Down

Take your chances or play it safe, you're under the order of the coin in this easy to grasp game where heads & tails meets drinking and gambling.

Hockey

A very original and exciting game for those who don't want to get off their arse but want a few tinnies, AKA beer hockey.

Irish Luck-Cup

A wonderfully simple game of luck that can be played with ease in any drinking environment, let the magic dice and coin decide who gets battered.

Landmines

A challenge of coin spinning skill with quick drinking involved. Make sure your coin does not hit the landmines to avoid the punishment drinks.

Quarters

The classic American-inspired game of coin and glass which can be played casually or competitively. Bounce the coin into the drink to make your opponents drink!

So board

As simple as flipping a coin onto paper. If it lands on a task it must be completed, if it lands on open space a new task is drawn for somebody else to land on.

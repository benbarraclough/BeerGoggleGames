Outdoor Games

Games made for outdoor spaces that usually involve throwing or actions not meant for indoors.

Browse the list

Select one of the games below to see further details on how to play

Beer Darts

A casual game of throwing darts at beer cans. Get some bullseyes by hitting an opponents can and making them drink. Beware of injuries!

Detonator

A brutal game for the hard-headed. Take it in turns to smash a can on your head as hard as possible, drink when it explodes.

Dizzy Bat

A simplified outdoor sports game that gets a lot of laughs. Can chugging required!

Flunky Ball

A German drinking team sport for those who like to get pissed in the sun as well as getting some exercise. Throw the ball at the targets and finish your drinks first to win.

Frisbeer

A game of precision frisbee using targets and alcohol so you don’t get bored after just a few throws.

Goon of Fortune

An outdoor Australian favourite using sacks of wine attached to a rotary clothing line to create a unique and suspenseful way of drinking.

No Eye Can Catch

A game that requires teamwork and spacial awareness. As simple as throw and catch, just try to avoid the concussion.

Polish Horseshoes

A fun frisbee game where you aim to hit the other teams can, perfect for the beach, camping or garden days in Summer.

Relay

A relay race requiring speed and agility in both drinking and running. Compete in teams and complete the obstacle course first to win the race.

singe

A game requiring fire safety and a precise lighter skills. Players can light the napkin as much as they wish but must not let the coin that is sitting on top fall through.

Slip 'n' Slide

The best way to play flip cup in summer, a relay race on a slip and slide over grass to a table with a load of drinks.

Stump

A legendary German drinking game stolen by the yanks. The game consists of hammer throwing and nail smashing in rapid succession.

Select one of the games below to see further details on how to play

1 2 3

A simple dice rolling game that gets you as drunk as you wish. The first two people who roll 6's prep the drink and the person who rolls the third 6 chugs it.

21

A knockout counting game that gradually moves around the circle. Each player strategically says from one to three numbers at a time to try and make sure that they aren't the one that says 21.

666

A fast paced game requiring full concentration. Watch the clock and roll the dice, make sure you arent holding the token when a 6 is rolled.

7 11 Doubles

An easy to understand and very entertaining game that tests a players' speed drinking and dice rolling abilities whilst others watch with joy.

Around The World

A fun social game that will get the drinking started, take it in turns to name countries starting with the same letter the previous answer ends in.

Arrogance

Take your chances or play it safe, you're under the dominion of the coin in this easy to grasp game, where heads & tails meets high-stakes drinking.

Baseball

An All-American team sport made easy, using elements of beer pong as the competitive aspect, hit some runs and don't get out.

Battle Shots

A DIY game for those who feel an impulse to be creative with how they drink. The classic game of Battleships with a drinking twist, you must be able to keep down those heavy spirits.

Beer Darts

A casual game of throwing darts at beer cans. Get some bullseyes by hitting an opponents can and making them drink. Beware of injuries!

Beer Die

Deep in the Alpha Tau Omega house at the University of Maine in 1972, four fraternity brothers discovered the legendary die throwing, point scoring, drink sinking game.

Beer Pong

The most legendary drinking game of all time originating from the Roman Empire when a stone fell from above into the chalice of Julius Caesar.

Black Black White

A fun hoax game to confuse your friends with and get everyone as drunk as you like before revealing the true nature of the game.

Blow Ball

Using the skill of blowing, navigate a ping pong ball through an obstacle course to glory as fast as possible.

Blow Cup

A game requiring composure where you must transport an empty cup to the finish by only using the blowing power of your mouth.

Blow Me

Often called Beer Blow or Bottle Blow, this game consists of blowing cards off the top of a drink delicately so that only some of the cards fall but not all.

Boat Race

A relay race of speed drinking, great for big groups! Quick, easy, competitive and never fails to get everyone involved and seriously pumped.

Bluff

A game of where luck and cunning come together, dish out drinks and make your contenders believe you.

Boxing

A quick-fire dice rolling game similar to a boxing match, testing your drinking endurance and rolling ability.

Can Slide

An alcoholics version of shuffleboard. Slide Cans into eachothers zones to make the other team drink but don't let the can fall off the end.

Chandelier

Throw the ball into your oppponents cups to make them drink. Get it in the wrong cup and you'll need to be good at flip cup to avoid the chug cup.

Civil War

A popular method of beer pong utilising more cups and more players to create a war-like scenario of constant shooting.

Corners

A quick and easy 4 player version of Beer Pong, whereby two ping pong balls are thrown in rapid succession by both teams to eliminate eachothers lives as quickly as possible.

Cross The Bridge

A fun yet frustrating game of chance and instinct where you must choose the correct cards consecutively to get across the table to your opponent.

Cup Toss

Similar to a game of ring toss at the fair, throw the solo cups onto the beer bottles to make the opposition drink.

Curse

A fast paced group game where you try to escape the curse by passing it onto someone else before time runs out!

Deceit

A game for the lying bastard in us all. Use deceit to fool your opponents into believing that you have or have not taken a shot to make them guess incorrectly.

Detonator

A brutal game for the hard-headed. Take it in turns to smash a can on your head as hard as possible, drink when it explodes.

Dick Head

Feel like verbally abusing your friends but need an excuse? This is the game for you! A simple yet exciting card game for all, similar to a game of snap.

Dizzy Bat

A simplified outdoor sports game that gets a lot of laughs. Can chugging required!

Don't Interrupt

A good old classroom game made fun with alcohol! Count together as a group for as long as you can, just don't mess up or clash with another player.

Doom

Play your cards wisely to avoid the big centre chug cup of doom. If not, hope that chance is in your favour allowing you to escape... otherwise you drink.

Drink While You Think

Know your celebrities to get yourself safely through this game, its name says it all - drink as you think of the next answer.

Drunk Jenga

Just like regular jenga but with a drunk twist, where all bricks have a secret task that can include dares, truths, drinking or something more sinister.

Electricity

A social yet moderately heavy drinking game where you drink when cards 'conduct'. Easy to play, brings the excitement and gets the sparks flying (pun intended).

Eleusis

Eleusis is a card game of logical thinking and deduction where one player chooses a secret rule and other players must follow.

Fat Boy

A simple yet tricky group game requiring quick thinking and a sober brain. A great game to laugh at those who can't think on the spot (no fat jokes involved).

Fingers On

A fan favourite, incredibly simple and great for groups of any size! All fingers are placed on the chug cup and everyone gets their chance to escape, the loser is the last one with a finger on the cup.

Fives

An incredibly diverse yet simple game for groups of any size! A good starter drinking game very similar to the fingers on game but without the cup.

Flip Cup

A game of speed and composure for teams of any size, simply flip the cup and hope you're teammates don't screw up.

Flunky Ball

A German drinking team sport for those who like to get pissed in the sun as well as getting some exercise. Throw the ball at the targets and finish your drinks first to win.

Frisbeer

A game of precision frisbee using targets and alcohol so you don’t get bored after just a few throws.

Fuck the Dealer

A game which can get players very drunk if unlucky, guess the cards on the top of the deck to make the dealer drink.

Fuzzy Duck

You must think before you speak. The game that makes prudish players scream out a string of obscenities and drink generous amounts of alcohol.

Game of Seven

A game for those who like a good challenge, work as a pair to get a full 7 bounces of the ball across the table without messing up.

Get Stacked

A competitive game for teams of two where drinking, stacking and unstacking cups is the way to win. Play it tournament style or head to head.

Give & Take

A game of truth and dare with an alcoholic twist. Make sure not to give to the wrong people, or else you'll be punished.

Goon of Fortune

An outdoor Australian favourite using sacks of wine attached to a rotary clothing line to create a unique and suspenseful way of drinking.

Hockey

A very original and exciting game for those who don't want to get off their arse but want a few tinnies, AKA beer hockey.

Hotseat

A game of mutual respect and trust. Get in the hotseat and share your thoughts and be rewarded for how satisfying they are to your peers.

Hungry Hungry Hippos

A childhood game repurposed for adult use, collect and consume as many cups from the centre just like the greedy hippo you are.

Ice Tray Blitz

An easy group game similar to quarters but involves spirit sipping, put an ice tray in the middle and bounce.

Ice Trayn

An obscure game of quick drinking by utilising straws to drink from ice trays as quickly as possible. Race to the finish in teams of 1 or more!

Irish Luck-Cup

A wonderfully simple game of luck that can be played with ease in any drinking environment, let the magic dice and coin decide who gets battered.

Jurassic Chug

A drinking game for those who have the spirit animal of a T-rex. Group into teams to pass, drink and flip cups only using the mouth. No hands allowed!

Knockout Pong

A game sourced from the USA, similar to a game of knockout basketball. Score your shots as quick as you can before the player behind you to stay in the game.

Landmines

A challenge of coin spinning skill with quick drinking involved. Make sure your coin does not hit the landmines to avoid the punishment drinks.

Liars Dice

Also known as Perudo, this famous dice game requires tactics, forward thinking and prediction to win, alongside a level of deception that may cause uproar.

Lucky Number

A dice rolling game for groups where you must roll your number to exit the game.

Majority Rules

Follow the Kings laws and legislations in this game of chance by guessing the Kings choices and staying within the majority as best as possible to retain your lives and win the game.

Millennium Bridge

Take it in turns to balance coins on the millennium bridge, but don't be the one that makes the bridge collapse.

Most Likely

A great conversation starting game that requires a quick reaction of brutal truth. Everyone takes turns to ask the group who is most likely of a certain action or eventuality.

Mushroom

Similar to the boardgame topple, take it in turns to stack the cards on top of the chug cup creating a mushroom. Don't be the one that makes the cards fall.

Naughts & Crosses

A game of naughts and crosses or tic tac toe with an alcoholic twist. This game encorporates quickfire flip cup, rewarding the fastest, most skilled flippers.

Never Have I Ever

This classic party starter is a drinking game in which players take turns asking other players about things they have not done, hoping to reveal some truths and get people drinking.

Nights At The Round Table

A luck based drinking game where various alcoholic drinks are dispersed around a table and the draw of a card determines who drinks which.

No Eye Can Catch

A game that requires teamwork and spacial awareness. As simple as throw and catch, just try to avoid the concussion.

Oblivion

A game of high risk requiring great nerve to compete and composure to perform. Complete the tasks before your opponent to trap them into oblivion. Do not play this game lightly!

Odd Or Even

Similar to the casino game "Craps", use your intuition to guess the outcome of the dice correctly to make others drink.

Pairs

A classic memory game turned fun with drink. Find the object pairs to create alcoholic concoctions for friends and participants.

Polish Horseshoes

A fun frisbee game where you aim to hit the other teams can, perfect for the beach, camping or garden days in Summer.

Pyramid Coin

A game of precision and quick throwing skills to get the coin in 3 different sized cups before your opponent, loser chugs.

Quarters

The classic American-inspired game of coin and glass which can be played casually or competitively. Bounce the coin into the drink to make your opponents drink!

Quick Flip

A fast paced 1v1 duel! Flip the cup over successfully to move the centre drink one step closer to your competitors side of the table whilst they do the same.

Race Car Shots

An excuse to get out your hot wheels collection. Accuracy is key in this brutal game of car rolling and shot necking.

Rage Cage

A high intensity group game of short range beer pong. Bounce it in the cup as quick as you can before being stacked by an opponent and suffering a tepid beer.

Relay

A relay race requiring speed and agility in both drinking and running. Compete in teams and complete the obstacle course first to win the race.

Ride The Bus

Ride the Bus is an excellent card drinking game that involves very simple gameplay and rules allowing participants to "learn as they play".

Ring Of Fire

An empty drinks container entrapped within a circle of 52 cards. Do not break the Ring of Fire! A great group game which works best with more people.

RingPong

A frantically fast paced beer pong game that involves quick shots and quick drinking whilst running round the table.

Scissors

A mystery party game where the rules of the game are hidden from playersand a pair of real or imaginary scissors are passed.

Shuffle Beer

A drinking alternative to shuffle board where cans are slid across the floor into zones to score maximum points, the losers drink their own cans. Can be played in teams or singles.

singe

A game requiring fire safety and a precise lighter skills. Players can light the napkin as much as they wish but must not let the coin that is sitting on top fall through.

Six Cups

Each cup has a designated number and each player rolls the dice. A game of chance will decide whether you will get to fill or drink from the cup.

Slap Shots

A simple yet brutal game for those who want a fraternity style of drinking that will take affect pretty quickly. Take a slap, take a shot, repeat.

Slip 'n' Slide

The best way to play flip cup in summer, a relay race on a slip and slide over grass to a table with a load of drinks.

So Board

As simple as flipping a coin onto paper. If it lands on a task it must be completed, if it lands on open space a new task is drawn for somebody else to land on.

Spoons

A brilliant game of quick reactions and pure excitement. Match a set of cards to grab a spoon and make it to the next round.

Stein Run

A race between teams of two or more consisting of rapid beer drinking and stein glass sliding, an absolute Après-ski favourite.

Strip Jack Drunk

An excitable card game of quick card flipping, similar to the famous strip jack naked card game but with an alcoholic twist.

Stump

A legendary German drinking game stolen by the yanks. The game consists of hammer throwing and nail smashing in rapid succession.

Suck & Blow

An intimately social card game where you must pass the card around the circle of players by only using the suction from your mouth.

Table of Terror

A game of spirit drinking terror that only ends once the table is empty. This game has no winner per se, but if you choose correctly, you just might not be a loser.

Tally Tally Aye

A socially involving game requiring a certain level of rhythm. Great for breaking the ice and having a laugh using gestures and incessant chanting.

Tealight Trial

An obscure yet challenging game that plays to the advantage of those with a strong blowing ability.

The Grand National

The most British drinking game of all which involves the betting, drinking and excitement of horse racing without the animal cruelty.

Three Man

Dice games at their best. Roll the dice and hope that it lands on a three just as long as you're not the Three Man.

Tinny

A very simple game which raises a lot of suspense. Take it in turns to place cards under the can tab until it cracks. Don't be the unlucky one!

Titanic

A fun challenge that demands a steady hand and will test some nerves. Pour a spirit into a shot glass (the Titanic) which floats on top of another drink, don't let it sink!

Tower

A game of chance and suspense. Create some dodgy drinks, make a tower, roll the dice and hope that you don't have to drink.

Unpopular Opinion

A game that raises interesting topics of conversation and punishes those with the worst opinions.

Wicked

A game of quick drinking and candle blowing, a one on one match where you strive to finish your drink before your opponent.

You Fuckin' Joker

A game of frantic swapsies where you must attempt to collect a set of cards whilst avoiding the joker.

Fingers On

An incredibly diverse yet simple game for groups of any size! A good introduction to a drink session or to stay entertained out at a venue

Beer Pong

The most legendary drinking game of all time originating from the Roman Empire when a stone fell from above into the chalice of Julius Caesar

Ring of Fire

An empty drinks container entrapped within a circle of 52 cards. Do not break the Ring of Fire!

Mushroom Cup

Similar to a game of jenga, stack the cards on top of a drink until they fall

Fat Boy

A simple yet tricky group game requiring quick thinking. Despite being incredibly funny, anti-obesity jokes are not encouraged

Battle Shots

A DIY game for those who feel an impulse to be creative with how they drink.

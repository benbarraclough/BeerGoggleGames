Mudslide

  1. Home
  2. Extras
  3. Recipes
  4. Mudslide

A coffee infused vodka cocktail, served ice cold for a refreshing pick-me-up.

Getting Started

Ingredients

  • Coffee liqueur
  • Vodka
  • Dark Chocolate
  • Ice
  • Irish cream liqueur
  • Double cream

Recipe

  1. Put chocolate in a shallow bowl and melt in the microwave in short bursts.
  2. Dip the rim of the chilled glasses in the melted chocolate, then stand them upright in the fridge so it gradually drips down the sides and cools.
  3. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice, then pour in the coffee-flavoured liqueur, vodka, Irish cream liqueur and double cream.
  4. Shake until the outside of the shaker is very cold.
  5. Put a few ice cubes in the prepared glasses, then strain in the cocktail. Finely grate over the remaining chocolate and serve with a paper straw.

# Forfeits

![Forfeits](/images/forfeits.webp)

A collection of some popular and creative forfeits that you can use as part of any drinking game.

> Have an idea? Use the feedback link below to suggest a new forfeit.

---

## Browse the list

(Consider converting these to collapsible sections later.)

### All Fours
Travel on all fours for a set amount of time.

### Beer Bong
Neck a full drink using a beer bong funnel and pipe.

### Cheese On Crackers
Shave a thin slice of soap off a bar of soap. Place it on a cracker and eat it.

### Chug Cup
Everyone donates a little of their drink into a glass until full. You must finish it.

### Dancing Monkey
For an agreed time you must dance whenever anyone demands it.

### Edward Ciderhands
Tape a cider bottle to each hand. Only when both are empty can you remove them.

### Dice of Doom
Pick a number. Roll until your number comes up 3 times; every other roll = drink.

### Disabled
Tape one limb; it must stay that way for the duration.

### Flirt
Flirt for a set time with a chosen person.

### Flour Power
Eat a spoonful of plain flour.

### Concussion
Open a can using only your head, then drink it. (Not recommended / safety risk.)

### Dilemma
Pick one “evil” from a prepared set of unpleasant shot/drink options.

### Flaming Shot
Ignite a high‑proof shot and (safely) consume it in one. Extreme caution.

### Go Live
Go live on a social app for 10 minutes with someone visible at all times.

### Graffiti
Another player draws on you anywhere with a marker.

### Ice-T
Wear a previously frozen soaked t‑shirt until thawed.

### Leash
A chosen “owner” keeps you on a belt/leash for a set period.

### Outcast
All players ignore you completely for the duration.

### Shit Shave
Someone of your choosing shaves a body area they pick.

### Shot Roulette
Multiple mystery shots; blind pick and drink.

### Sock It
Drink a beverage through someone’s (clean-ish) sock stretched over the rim.

### Spinning Mad
Spin a coin; stop it with one finger (right hand once, left hand once) — keep trying until both succeed.

### The Salon
Someone applies fake tan to a body part you present.

### Toilet Troll
Loiter in a bathroom offering “help” to others without using the facilities.

### TP
Eat one square of toilet paper.

### Winters Coming
Put on three extra clothing layers for a set time.

### 50 Shades
Seductively read a chosen passage of literature to someone.

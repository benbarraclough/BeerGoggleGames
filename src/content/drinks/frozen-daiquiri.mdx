<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A frosty favourite with fruit and ice, perfect for a warm day drink in the summer sun.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 10 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>

  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Cuba</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>White rum - 50ml</li>
  <li>Fruit of choice - 250g</li>
  <li>Ice - 100g</li>
  <li>Lime - 15ml</li>
  <li>Simple syrup - 15ml</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Blend your chosen fruit into a puree (strawberries in this instance) and sieve out the unwanted bits.</li>
  <li>Blend again the fruit puree with the ice, the rum, lime and sugar syrup.</li>
  <li>Pour the contents into a glass, garnish with a few pieces of fruit and add a straw.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>You can use frozen fruit to give your drinks a thicker texture.</li>
    <li>Don't blend for too long, or else your misture will become too watery.</li>
    <li>Blending your mixture with crushed ice is better than big chunks.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A very Irish looking shot with a bold mint green colour and flavour, its refreshing, creamy and rich with a kick.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 4 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> Dairy</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Ireland</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Vodka - 20ml</li>
  <li>Creme de Menthe - 20ml</li>
  <li>Cream - 30ml</li>
  <li>Chocolate bitters - 2 dashes</li>
  <li>Whipped cream - 1 squirt (optional)</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the creme de menthe, vodka, cream and chocolate bitters into a shaker with ice.</li>
  <li>Shake until the shaker turns ice cold.</li>
  <li>Strain into a shot glass.</li>
  <li>Top with whipped cream.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>If available, an AfterEight chocolate works well as a side snack.</li>
    <li>The best cream to use for this drink would be heavy cream, but any should do.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

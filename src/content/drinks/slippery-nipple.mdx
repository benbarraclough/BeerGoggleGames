<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Grenadine - 15ml</li>
  <li>Sambuca - 15ml</li>
  <li>Irish Cream Liqueur - 15ml</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the Grenadine in the bottom of th glass</li>
  <li>Follow this up with pouring the sambuca in gently so it sits on top of the grenadine</li>
  <li>Finish it off by pouring the Irish cream liqueur gently on top</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Use a spoon to pour each element in gently so that it layers perfectly</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Gin - 25ml</li>
  <li>Benedictine - 10ml</li>
  <li>Grand Marnier - 10ml</li>
  <li>Cherry liqueur - 10ml</li>
  <li>Pineapple juice - 35ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 15ml</li>
  <li>Angostura bitters - 2 dashes</li>
  <li>Soda water - to fill</li>
  <li>Orange - 1</li>
  <li>Cherry - 1</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the gin, Benedictine, Grand Marnier, cherry liqueur, pineapple juice, lime juice and bitters into a shaker with ice.</li>
  <li>Shake well until the shaker turns ice cold.</li>
  <li>Strain into a glass over fresh ice, and top up with the soda water.</li>
  <li>Garnish with orange slices and a cherry</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>The Benedictine can be substituted for Chartreuse or Drambuie if it is not available.</li>
    <li>The Grand Marnier can be substituted for Cointreau if it is not available.</li>
    <li>A Maraschino cherry it ideal for this drink</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

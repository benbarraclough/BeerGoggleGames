<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Vodka - 25ml</li>
  <li>Gin - 25ml</li>
  <li>Tequila - 25ml</li>
  <li>White rum - 25ml</li>
  <li>Triple sec - 25ml</li>
  <li>Lemon juice - 25ml</li>
  <li>Cola - 150ml</li>
  <li>Ice - Handful</li>
  <li>Lemon (optional) - Two slices</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour each spirit and the liqueur into a shaker with a handful of ice, shake until the outside of the shaker is very cold.</li>
  <li>Strain the mixture into a glass and add the lemon juice.</li>
  <li>Fill to the top with cola and add a slice of lemon into the drink</li>
  <li>Garnish with another slice of lemon and take a sip with a straw</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Try to use high quality spirits to reduce the impact of the hangover.</li>
    <li>Replace the lemon slices and lemon juice with lime for a slightly different taste.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A twist on the traditional tequila shot, flavouring the wedge of lime instead of the shot itself.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 3 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Tequila - 50ml</li>
  <li>Bitters - 10ml</li>
  <li>Black pepper - 1 sprinkle</li>
  <li>Lime - 1</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour out the tequila in the shot glass.</li>
  <li>Mix together the bitters and black pepper on a plate.</li>
  <li>Cut a wedge out of the lime and soak the flesh in the bitters and black pepper mixture.</li>
  <li>Balance the lime on the top of the shot glass for presentation.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>For a weeter taste, add sugar to the bitters and black pepper mixture.</li>
    <li>An alternative flavour for the lime would be to use tajin</li>
    <li>Aged tequila will go down smoother (Reposado or Anejo)</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

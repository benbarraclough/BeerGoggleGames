  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A sweet yet spiced cocktail for those who like their dark rum in a long drink.</p>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Dark rum - 25ml</li>
  <li>Ginger Ale - 100ml</li>
  <li>Lime - 10ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add a few cubes ice to a glass along with a squeeze off lime juice</li>
  <li>Pour as many shots of dark rum as you'd like</li>
  <li>Fill to the top with ginger ale mixer</li>
  <li>Mix with a stirer and garnish with a wedge of lime</li>
  <li>Further garnish of rosemary or mint leaf can be used for extra pizzazz alongside a small amount of grated cinnamon stick.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

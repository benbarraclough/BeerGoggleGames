<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Vodka - 120ml</li>
  <li>Jelly mixture - 1 packet</li>
  <li>Water - 360ml</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>The following recipe makes enough for 12 jello shots.</li>
  <li>Boil 240ml of water in a kettle.</li>
  <li>Add the jelly mixture into a jug and pour the boiling water on top.</li>
  <li>Mix the jelly mixture and water until dissolved.</li>
  <li>Once dissolved pour in the vodka and remaining 120ml of cold water then mix.</li>
  <li>Pour the mixture into shot glasses or tubs.</li>
  <li>Place these containers on a tray and refridgerate for a minimum of 2 hours.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>The recipe may vary dependent on the mixture used, see the back of whtever packet you use.</li>
    <li>In this recipe, a quarter of the liquid used is alcohol. Increase the amount for a stronger shot.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

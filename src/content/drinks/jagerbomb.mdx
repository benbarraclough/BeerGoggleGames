<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Jägermeister - 50ml</li>
  <li>Energy drink (Redbull) - 100ml</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the jägermeister in the shot section of the glass</li>
  <li>Pour the energy drink in the outer part of the glass to the top. The jäger should stay in place as it is heavier than the carbonated energy drink.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>If you don't have a Jägerbomb glass, drop a shot glass into a regular glass.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

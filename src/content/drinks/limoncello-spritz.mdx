<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A refreshing cocktail that combines the zesty flavour of lemon and the bubbly vibrance of prosecco.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 3 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Wine glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Italy</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Limoncello - 40ml</li>
  <li>Prosecco - 100ml</li>
  <li>Soda water - 50ml</li>
  <li>Lemon - 1</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the ice to a glass, then pour in the prosecco and limoncello.</li>
  <li>Fill to the top with soda water and mix.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a slice of lemon.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>To make for a stronger flavour, add less soda water or replace the soda with lemonade.</li>
    <li>If the limoncello is weak in alcohol percentage, give the drink an extra kick by adding some vodka.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A gin based variation of the Manhattan which has deep flavours and fruity accents.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Coupe glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> California, USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Gin - 50ml</li>
  <li>Vermouth Rosso - 50ml</li>
  <li>Cherry liqueur - 15ml</li>
  <li>Angostura bitters - 2 dashes</li>
  <li>Orange (optional)</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the gin, vermouth, cherry liqueur and bitters into a mixing glass with ice and stir until well-chilled.</li>
  <li>Strain into a chilled glass</li>
  <li>Cut some orange peel to make an orange twist</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Extra dry gin is traditionally used for this drink, however if you want a sweeter drink, regular gin can be used just the same.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

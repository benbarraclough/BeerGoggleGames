<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Vodka - 25ml</li>
  <li>Triple sec - 15ml</li>
  <li>lime juice - 10ml</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the vodka in the shot glass</li>
  <li>Add the triple sec</li>
  <li>Finish with the lime juice</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Turn the shot into a blue Kamikaze by replacing the Triple sec with Blue Curacao</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

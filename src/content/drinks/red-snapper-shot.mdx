<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A bit more palatable than the similarly named cocktail, utilising whisky and a liqueur instead of gin and tomato juice.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 3 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> Nuts</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Whisky - 20ml</li>
  <li>Amaretto - 20ml</li>
  <li>Cranberry juice - 40ml</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the whisky, amaretto and juice into a shaker with ice.</li>
  <li>Shake until the shaker turns ice cold.</li>
  <li>Pour into a shot glass and serve.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>A sweet whisky is preferred for this shot, traditionally a Canadian whisky is used, alterantively use a bourbon.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A classic cocktail known for its refreshing and tangy character, offering balanced sweet and botanical flavours.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 3 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Coupe glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> UK</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Gin - 60ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 25ml</li>
  <li>Simple syrup - 25ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Lime - 1</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Combine the gin, syrup and juice in a shaker with ice.</li>
  <li>Shake until the shaker is ice cold</li>
  <li>Strain into a glass and garnish with a lime wedge.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Make your own lime infused syrup by combining equal parts sugar and boiling water, them steeping the rind of lime within the mixture.</li>
    <li>Freshly squeezed lime juice is preferred.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

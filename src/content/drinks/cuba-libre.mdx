<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> One of the simplest cocktails to make, no equipment required. This drink combines just three ready-made ingredients.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 2 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Highball glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Cuba</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Rum - 50ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 15ml</li>
  <li>Cola - 150ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Lime - 1 (optional)</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add ice, vodka and lime juice to a glass</li>
  <li>Top up with a fizzy cola drink.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a few slices or a wedge of lime and stir.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Vary the type of rum to change the taste of the drink (e.g. spiced, flavoured, dark, light)</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

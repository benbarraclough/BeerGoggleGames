<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A hot and spicy shot for adventurous drinkers that don't mind a kick.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 3 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> America</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Vodka - 50ml</li>
  <li>Hot sauce - 2 dashes</li>
  <li>Chilli powder - 1 tsp</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Rub the rim of the shot glass with a lime and dip the rim in chilli powder</li>
  <li>Pour the hot sauce in the bottom of the shot glass</li>
  <li>Fill to the top with vodka. Pouring this in last should mix the chilli into the vodka</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Add hot sauce based on preference</li>
    <li>Use chilli flakes or shaved chilli to add a little more heat</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Tequila - 50ml</li>
  <li>Lime - 1 wedge</li>
  <li>Salt - 1 pinch</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Fill the shot glass to the top with tequila of any kind</li>
  <li>Cut a lime into wedges</li>
  <li>Take a pinch of salt and sit it on the back of your hand</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Proceed by licking th salt off the back of your hand, taking the shot and finally sucking the flesh of the lime wedge in this order.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A simple yet versatile concoction of beer and whisky dating back to the early 1800s.</p>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Whisky - 50ml</li>
  <li>Beer - 1 pint</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour your pint of beer as full as you'd like (expect overspill if you fill it to the top)</li>
  <li>Pour your full shot of whisky</li>
  <li>Simply drop the shot of whisky into the pint and leave it as it lies</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>The pint/shot is usually drank in one</li>
    <li>Combine different types of whisky with different types of beer for new flavours (e.g. Bourbon & IPA, Irish & Lager, Scotch & Ale)</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

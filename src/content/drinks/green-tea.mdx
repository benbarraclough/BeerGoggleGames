<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A quick and easy shot to make that uses a base of whisky and a zesty hit of fruit juice replicating a green tea.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Ireland</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Whisky (Irish) - 15ml</li>
  <li>Peach schnapps - 15ml</li>
  <li>Lemon juice - 5ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 5ml</li>
  <li>Simple syrup - 5ml</li>
  <li>Water - 5ml</li>
  <li>Lemon & lime soda (Sprite) - To fill</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Create a sour mix by muddling together the lemon juice, lime juice, simple syrup and water.</li>
  <li>Pour this mixture into the shot glass</li>
  <li>Add equal amounts whisky and peach schnapps trying to keep them the same as the sour mixture</li>
  <li>Top off with lemon and lime soda if needed</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>To make sure the sour mix is made correctly, make more than enough and test it. You should be able to taste the sourness and the sweetness equally.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A traditionally non-alcoholic cocktail made with vodka, sweet and refreshing usually made with ginger ale.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Highball glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Vodka - 50ml</li>
  <li>Ginger ale - 100ml</li>
  <li>Grenadine - 10ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 15ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Maraschino cherry - 1 (optional)</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add ice, vodka, lime juice and grenadine to a shaker.</li>
  <li>Shake until ice cold then pour into a glass with ice.</li>
  <li>Fill to the top with ginger ale or your chosen mixer.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a maraschino cherry.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>For more of an alcohol kick, use triple sec in place of the lime juice.</li>
    <li>Replace the plain vodka with cherry vodka, vanilla vodka, or whipped vodka for added flavor.</li>
    <li>Swap out the lemon-lime soda with some ginger ale, or do a 50/50 combo.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

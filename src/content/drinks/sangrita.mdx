<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A traditional Mexican shot served as a chaser alongside tequila, intending to cleanse and brighten the palate.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Hard</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Mexico</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Tequila - 50ml</li>
  <li>Tomato juice - 25ml</li>
  <li>Orange juice - 10ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 10ml</li>
  <li>Tabasco - 2 dashes</li>
  <li>Ground black pepper - 2 pinches</li>
  <li>Salt - 1 pinch</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Fill one shot glass to the brim with the tequila</li>
  <li>Fill a shaker with the tomato juice, orange juice, lime juice, tabasco, pepper, salt and ice.</li>
  <li>Shake until the shaker turns ice cold</li>
  <li>Pour into a second shot glass</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>A shaker is not explicitly needed, however makes sure all ingredients are well mixed as this is tricky to do in a shot glass.</li>
    <li>The tequila should be drank before the sangrita shot which acts as the chaser.</li>
    <li>Grenadine can be added to make the sangrita more sweet</li>
    <li>Grapefruit can be added to make the sangrita more tart</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A spiced vodka based drink with a hint of lime commonly served ice cold in a copper mug.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Copper Mug</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Russia</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Vodka - 50ml</li>
  <li>Ginger Beer - 150ml</li>
  <li>Lime Juice - 25ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Mint - 1 sprig (optional)</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>1\. Add a handful of crushed ice to the mug</li>
  <li>2\. Pour in the vodka & lime then mix</li>
  <li>3\. Fill to the top with ginger beer and top with mint as garnish</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Use ginger bitters to increase the spiced flavours in the drink</li>
    <li>Ginger ale can be an easy replacement for ginger beer</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A traditional whisky cocktail that can be drank neat or with soda.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Rocks glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Kentucky, USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Whisky - 60ml</li>
  <li>Angostura bitters - 1 dash</li>
  <li>Sugar syrup - 2 tsp</li>
  <li>Orange - 1 Slice</li>
  <li>Water - 1 Splash</li>
  <li>Ice</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Put the syrup, bitters and water into a glass.</li>
  <li>Add the whisky and stir until mixed.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a slice of orange.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>The whisky can be replaced with a bourbon</li>
    <li>If you want a long drink instead of neat, add soda water</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

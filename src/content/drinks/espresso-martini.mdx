<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Coffee liqueur - 25ml</li>
  <li>Vodka - 25ml</li>
  <li>Espresso coffee - 25ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Simple syrup (demerara) - 15ml</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Mix equal parts caster sugar and boiling water in a pan, stir and bring to the boil.</li>
  <li>Turn the heat off and let the mixture cool.</li>
  <li>Add a handful of ice, 1 part vodka, 1 part coffee liqueur, 1 part espresso coffee and a teaspoon of cooled sugar syrup to a cocktail shaker.</li>
  <li>Shake until the outside of the cocktail shaker feels icy cold.</li>
  <li>Pour evenly into the chilled glasses and garnish with coffee beans.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Place glasses in the fridge before preparing the drink.</li>
    <li>When adding all of the ingredients in the shaker, pour the coffee in last to reduce the ice from melting as quickly.</li>
    <li>Replace espresso coffee with cream to create a white russian.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

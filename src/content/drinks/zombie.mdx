<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A tiki cocktail combining fruit juices, liqueurs, and various rums, founded in Hollywood in the 1930's.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Zombie glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> California, USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Dark rum</li>
  <li>White rum</li>
  <li>Lime juice</li>
  <li>Pineapple juice</li>
  <li>Grenadine</li>
  <li>Ice</li>
  <li>Orange (optional)</li>
  <li>Cherry (optional)</li>
  <li>Mint (optional)</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the rums and fruit juices into a cocktail shaker filled with ice and shake hard until the outside of the shaker feels really cold.</li>
  <li>Strain the mixture into a tall glass or hurricane glass filled with ice, then pour in the grenadine to colour the drink.</li>
  <li>Add the orange, cherry and mint to garnish</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Skewer the fruit garnish for a more elegant garnish</li>
    <li>Slowly pour the grenadine onto a spoon to gently add it to the drink so that the drink has a colour gradient.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

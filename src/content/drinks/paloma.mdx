<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Tequila - 50ml</li>
  <li>Grapefruit juice - 60ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 10ml</li>
  <li>Honey - 10ml</li>
  <li>Soda water - To fill</li>
  <li>Salt</li>
  <li>Ice - 2 handfuls</li>
  <li>Grapefruit (optional) - 1 Slice</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour some salt onto a plate, wet the rim of the glass with lime juice and dip the rim in the salt to cover it.</li>
  <li>Fill a cocktail shaker with the tequila, juices, honey and ice then shake until the shaker feels ice cold.</li>
  <li>Add a handful of ice to the glass and then pour in the strained mixture.</li>
  <li>Top up with soda water and garnish with a slice of grapefruit.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>If you prefer a sweeter cocktail, use more simple syrup and cover the rim in granulated sugar</li>

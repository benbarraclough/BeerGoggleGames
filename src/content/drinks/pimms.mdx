<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A refreshing fruit filled punch drink that epitomises summer time drinking in the UK.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Highball glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> England</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pimms - 50ml</li>
  <li>Lemonade - 100ml</li>
  <li>Mixed fruits - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Cucumber - 1 slice</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Mint - a few leaves</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Chop up the mixed fruits (strawberry, orange, apple, lemon) and slice a cucumber strip so that its all prepared.</li>
  <li>Get your tall glass and fill it with ice.</li>
  <li>Add the Pimms and lemonade along with the fruit & cucumber.</li>
  <li>Give it a mix, add the mint and pop a straw in to top it off</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Keep the fruit chunks big so they don't get stuck in straws.</li>
    <li>Make enough for all using a punch bowl or a dispenser, just multiply the recipe by however many drinks you want it to make.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A simple yet interesting cocktail with a whisky base and flavours of berries.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Coupe glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Ireland</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Whisky (rye) - 50ml</li>
  <li>Grapefruit juice - 25ml</li>
  <li>raspberry syrup - 20ml</li>
  <li>Raspberries (to garnish) - 3</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the whisky, juice, syrup and ice to a shaker.</li>
  <li>Shake until ice cold</li>
  <li>Strain in the glass</li>
  <li>Garnish with raspberries using a skewer</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Most whiskys can be used for this drink but rye whisky is famously used in this drink to add the spiced flavour to the drink.</li>
    <li>To add a little fizz to the drink, grapefuit soda can be used instead of juice.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> An old but popular classic dating back to the early 1900s, a deep yet sweet orange flavour.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Coupe glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> England</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Brandy - 60ml</li>
  <li>Orange liqueur (Triple Sec) - 30ml</li>
  <li>Lemon juice - 30ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the brandy, orange liqueur, lemon juice and ice to a mixer</li>
  <li>Shake the mixture until the shaker becomes ice cold</li>
  <li>Strain the mixture into the glass</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>If brandy isn't available, it can be replaced with a bourbon or a smooth blended whisky alonside a little simple syrup</li>
    <li>Garnish with an orange twist</li>
    <li>Freshly squeezed lemon juice is always preferred</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

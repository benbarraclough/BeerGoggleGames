<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A classy drink that is easy to make using just vodka and vermouth, a favourite amongst cocktail enthusiasts.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 2 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Martini glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Vodka - 90ml</li>
  <li>Vermouth Bianco - 30ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Olive - 1</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the vodka and vermouth to a shaker with the ice</li>
  <li>Shake until ice cold and strain into a glass</li>
  <li>Skewer an olive to garnish and place on top</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>To make it a dirty martini, add some olive juice.</li>
    <li>If you don't like olives, use lemon rind to garnish</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

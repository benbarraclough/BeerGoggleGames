<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A rich whisky based delight, its dark and aromatic flavours make it a very satisying drink.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Coupe glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Trinidad & Tobago</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Whisky - 15ml</li>
  <li>Angostura bitters - 45ml</li>
  <li>Almond liqueur - 30ml</li>
  <li>Lemon juice - 25ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the bitters, whisky, almond liqueur, lemon juice and ice to a shaker</li>
  <li>Shake the mixture until the shaker turns ice cold</li>
  <li>Strain into the glass</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Garnish with a lemon twist</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

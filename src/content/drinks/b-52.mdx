<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A multitude of sweet and creamy liqueurs layered in the glass in order of most dense to lease dense.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 3 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Canada</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Irish cream liqueur - 15ml</li>
  <li>Coffee liqueur - 15ml</li>
  <li>Grand Marnier liqueur - 15ml</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>First pour the coffee liqueur in the bottom of the glass</li>
  <li>Slowly pour the Irish cream liqueur on top</li>
  <li>Gently top with Grand marnier so that each liqueur has a separate and equal share of the shot glass</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>The best alternative to Grand marnier is Cointreau, similar in taste and alcohol percentage.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Coffee liqueur - 25ml</li>
  <li>Vodka - 25ml</li>
  <li>Dark Chocolate - 25g</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Irish cream liqueur - 25ml</li>
  <li>Double cream - 50ml</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Chill a glass by putting it in the fridge.</li>
  <li>Whilst the glass chills, put the chocolate in a shallow bowl and melt in the microwave in short bursts.</li>
  <li>Dip the rim of the chilled glass in the melted chocolate, then stand it upright in the fridge so it gradually drips down the sides and cools.</li>
  <li>Fill a cocktail shaker with ice, then pour in the coffee liqueur, vodka, Irish cream liqueur and double cream.</li>
  <li>Shake until the outside of the shaker is very cold.</li>
  <li>Get the glass out and put a few ice cubes in, then strain in the cocktail. Finely grate over the remaining chocolate and serve with a straw.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Make sure the chocolate has chilled for long enough in the fridge</li>
    <li>Use a shallow bowl for your chocolate so its easy to dip your glasses into</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

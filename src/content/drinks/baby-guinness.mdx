<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A smooth and creamy delight, the only relation it has to a true Guinness is its appearance.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 1 min</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> Lactose</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Ireland</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Coffee liqueur (Kahlua) - 35ml</li>
  <li>Irish cream liqueur (Baileys) - 15ml</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Start with the coffee liqueur, filling the shot glass 70% of the way.</li>
  <li>Fill to the top with the Irish cream liqueur, pouring it slowly so that it sits on top of the coffee liqueur</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Use a spoon when pouring in the Irish cream liqueur to make sure it sits on top and doesn't mix with the coffee liqueur</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

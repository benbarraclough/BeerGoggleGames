<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A whisky shot with a sweet yet sharp twist, utilising pickle brine as the chaser.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 2 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> 2 shot glasses</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Whisky - 50ml</li>
  <li>Pickle juice - 50ml</li>
  <li>Pickle - 1 (optional)</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the whisky into the first shot glass.</li>
  <li>Pour the pickle juice into the second shot glass.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a slice or minature pickle on top.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>The traditional recipe was made with Jameson Irish Whiskey but any can be used.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

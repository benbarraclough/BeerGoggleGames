<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A drink that tastes of pure paradise, a sweet and tropical tiki cocktail that encorporates 2 types of rum.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Rocks glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>White rum - 50ml</li>
  <li>Dark rum - 15ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 25ml</li>
  <li>Orange liqueur - 25ml</li>
  <li>Almond liqueur - 15ml</li>
  <li>Grenadine - 10ml</li>
  <li>Crushed ice - 2 handfuls</li>
  <li>Mint (optional) - 1 sprig</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the white rum, lime juice, orange liqueur, almond liqueur and crushed ice to a shaker</li>
  <li>Shake lightly for a few seconds and pour the mixture unstrained into the glass</li>
  <li>Top the drink off with the dark rum and then the grenadine. This should give the drink a cascading colour change.</li>
  <li>Add the mint sprig to complete</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Make sure the crushed ice isn't partially melted as this will water own the drink</li>
    <li>The drink can just be stirred in, however a quick allows the multiple flavours of the spirits and liqueurs to meld better</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

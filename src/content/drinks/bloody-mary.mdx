<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Vodka - 50ml</li>
  <li>Tomato juice - 100ml</li>
  <li>Lemon juice - 10ml</li>
  <li>Worcestershire sauce - 2 Dashes</li>
  <li>Tobasco - 2 Dashes</li>
  <li>Celery salt - 1 Pinch</li>
  <li>Black pepper - 1 Pinch</li>
  <li>Ice - 2 Handfuls</li>
  <li>Celery - 1 Stick</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add all of the ingredients except the celery stick to a shaker</li>
  <li>Stir or shake gently to mix the ingredients without making the mixture frothy</li>
  <li>Add ice to the glass and pour in the mixture</li>
  <li>Crush the celery stick a little to make the juices flow and drop it in to finish</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Add more spice where necessary if you like a stronger kick</li>
    <li>You can add a lemon or lime slice as a garnish</li>
    <li>Add paprika or horseraddish if you want to get even more experimental with the flavours</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

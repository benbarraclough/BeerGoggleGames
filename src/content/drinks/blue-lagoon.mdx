<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> Similar to a sweet blue lemonade but with more alcohol and a hint of cherry.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Hurricane glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Paris, France</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Vodka - 50ml</li>
  <li>Lemonade - 200ml</li>
  <li>Blue Curaçao - 50ml</li>
  <li>Cherry syrup - 1 tsp</li>
  <li>Cherries - 1 Cherry</li>
  <li>Lemons - 1 Slice</li>
  <li>Ice</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add vodka, blue Curaçao and ice to a shaker.</li>
  <li>Shake the shaker until it turns very cold</li>
  <li>Strain the shaker contents into a glass with ice and top up with lemonade</li>
  <li>Pour the cherry syrup on top of the drink, then garnish with cherry and lemon.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Use fresh lemon, lime and orange juice along with soda water instead of lemonade</li>
    <li>If possible, use maraschino cherries and the syrup from them insetad of regular cherries</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

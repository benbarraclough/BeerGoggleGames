  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A pleasant tropical flavoured shot with lots of different fruits combined to make a low strength enjoyable shot.</p>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Coconut rum - 15ml</li>
  <li>Blue Curacao - 15ml</li>
  <li>Peach Schnapps - 15ml</li>
  <li>Sweet and Sour mix - 15ml</li>
  <li>Lemon-lime soda - 1 splash</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the rum, blue curacao, schnapps and sweet-sour mix into a shaker with ice.</li>
  <li>Shake until ice cold and then strain into a shot glass.</li>
  <li>Top off with sprite or another lemon-lime soda then serve.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Do not shake with the sprite in the shaker, this will bubble up and ruin the lemon-lime soda.</li>
    <li>Ensure equal parts ingredients.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

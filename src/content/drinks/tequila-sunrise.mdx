<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Tequila - 50ml</li>
  <li>Triple Sec - 20ml</li>
  <li>Orange juice - 100ml</li>
  <li>Grenadine - 10ml</li>
  <li>Lemon juice - 15ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Cherry (optional) - 1</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the grenadine into the bottom of the glass and set it aside.</li>
  <li>Add the tequila, triple sec, fruit juice and ice to a shaker.</li>
  <li>Shake until ice cold.</li>
  <li>Add fresh ice to the glass.</li>
  <li>Strain mixed drink into the glass.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a slice of orange and a cherry.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>A maraschino cherry is the preferred type to use for this drink.</li>
    <li>The grenadine can be poured in last for the waterfall effect, as it still rests on the bottom due to its higher density.</li>
    <li>If the drink is not entirely full, just fill further with orange juice.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

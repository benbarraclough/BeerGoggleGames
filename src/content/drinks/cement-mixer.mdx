<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Irish cream liqueur - 35ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 15ml</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the Irish cream liqueur</li>
  <li>Pour the lime juice on top using a spoon</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Pour the lime juice as gently as possible so that the flavour of lime is the first to hit the tongue. These liquids will likely blend, just do as best as you can.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

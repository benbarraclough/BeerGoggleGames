  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A simple yet refreshing gin spritz, the perfect drink for summer evenings.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Wine glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Italy</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Gin - 25ml</li>
  <li>Elderflower cordial - 25ml</li>
  <li>Soda - 25ml</li>
  <li>Prosecco - 125ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Mint - 1 sprig</li>
  <li>Lime - 1 wedge</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Start by slightly bruising the mint and placing it at the bottom of glass</li>
  <li>Pour the cordial and gin in the glass and leave it to infuse for 1-2 minutes</li>
  <li>Fill the glass with ice, then add the soda and prosecco</li>
  <li>Stir and add the lime wedge to finish</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>For a stronger taste, replace the soda with more cordial and gin</li>
    <li>Prosecco can just be used to fill the drink to the top, the exact measurement is not needed</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

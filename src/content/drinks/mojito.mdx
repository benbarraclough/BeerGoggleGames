<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>White rum - 25ml</li>
  <li>Soda water - 100ml</li>
  <li>Lime - 10ml</li>
  <li>Granulated sugar - 1tsp</li>
  <li>Mint - 2 handfuls</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Muddle the lime juice, sugar and 1 handful of mint leaves together in a shaker using a cocktail muddler, the aim is to bash and grind the sugar into the mint.</li>
  <li>Add the shot of rum and a couple cubes of ice, then shake until the shaker is ice cold.</li>
  <li>Strain the mixture into an ice filled glass along with the other handful of mint leaves (unbashed), then fill to the top with soda water.</li>
  <li>Stir and garnish with mint leaf and a slice of lime on top to serve.</li>
  <li>Add more of whatever ingredient you need to suit your own taste.</li>
  <li>Add different fresh fruits and syrups to easily create flavoured mojitos (strawberry, pineapple etc.)</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A stomach full of the Irish charm with an extra kick, drop it in and chug it down.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 2 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Pint & shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Ireland</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Guinness - 3/4 pint</li>
  <li>Irish whisky - 25ml</li>
  <li>Irish cream liqueur - 25ml</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the three quarters of a pint into a pint glass</li>
  <li>Pour the whisky and the Irish cream liqueur into the shot glass</li>
  <li>Drop the shot glass into the pint glass</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Pour the Guinness against the side of the glass so that it doesn't foam in the glass. When the shot is dropped in, more foam will be made.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Vodka - 30ml</li>
  <li>Grenadine - 10ml</li>
  <li>Pineapple juice - 30ml</li>
  <li>Cherry - 1 (optional)</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the vodka and pineapple juice into a shaker with ice.</li>
  <li>Shake until ice cold and strain into a shot glass.</li>
  <li>Pour the grenadine over and serve with a cherry on top.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>A maraschino cherry is the type to use for this drink.</li>
    <li>Vanilla vodka is preferred in this drink to give it a little more flavour.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

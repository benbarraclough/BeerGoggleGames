<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A sweet and creamy concoction which is basically a boozy milkshake, with tastes of chocolate, coconut and coffee.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Highball glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> Coffee</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> U.S. Virgin Islands</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Dark rum - 60ml</li>
  <li>Coffee liqueur - 30ml</li>
  <li>Creme de cacao - 30ml</li>
  <li>Milk - 60ml</li>
  <li>Cream of coconut - 30ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Nutmeg (optional)</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the dark rum, coffee liqueur, creme de cacao, milk, cream of coconut and a handful of ice into a blender.</li>
  <li>Blend until combined and frothy.</li>
  <li>Pour mixture into the glass and garnish with a sprinkle of nutmeg.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>The higher fat content of the milk used, the thicker and richer the drink.</li>
    <li>Chocolate bitters can be used in place of the chocolate liqueur but the drink may need to be sweetened with a little simple syrup.</li>
    <li>A hurricane glass is often used for this drink and can be decorated with a little chocolate sauce on the walls of the glass.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A provocatively named shot layered with a creamy combination of liqueurs and topped with whipped cream.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 3 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> Almonds</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Almond liqueur - 30ml</li>
  <li>Irish cream liqueur - 10ml</li>
  <li>Whipped cream</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the almond liqueur in the bottom of the shot glass (preferably Amaretto)</li>
  <li>Gently pour the Irish cream liqueur on top</li>
  <li>Top the drink off with whipped cream</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>After a certain amount of time, the whipped cream will sink into the drink, so drink quickly after making the shot.</li>
    <li>Use a bar spoon to pour the Irish cream liqueur on top so the shot has layers.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A sweet whisky shot with flavours of apple, originally sourced from prize winning orchards in 1980s Washington.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 3 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Whisky (Canadian) - 15ml</li>
  <li>Apple Schnapps - 15ml</li>
  <li>Cranberry Juice - 15ml</li>
  <li>Soda - to fill</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the Whisky (preferably Canadian), Schnapps and juice to a shaker with ice</li>
  <li>Shake until shaker feels cold</li>
  <li>Pour into a shot glass</li>
  <li>Fill to the top with soda</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Serving chilled is best when enjoying this drink, however if the cranberry juice has been refridgerated then it doesnt need to be shaken.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

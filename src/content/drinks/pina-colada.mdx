<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>White rum - 50ml</li>
  <li>Coconut cream - 50ml</li>
  <li>Pineapple juice - 100ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 2 handfuls</li>
  <li>Pineapple (optional) - 1</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the rum, coconut cream, pineapple juice and a handful of ice into a blender.</li>
  <li>Blend until smooth and then pour into a glass with ice.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a slice of pineapple and a leaf or two.</li>
  <li>If you don't have a blender at hand, crush the ice manually somehow and mix everything together using a cocktail shaker.</li>
  <li>Coconut cream and cream of coconut are used to make the best piña coladas, however coconut milk can be used as well. Coconut milk is a little less rich in flavour and unsweetened, keep this in mind.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

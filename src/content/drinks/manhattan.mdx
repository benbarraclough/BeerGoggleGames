<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A classic whisky based cocktail offering a rich and balanced flavour thats not too sweet.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 3 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Martini glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Whisky (rye) - 60ml</li>
  <li>Vermouth rosso - 30ml</li>
  <li>Angostura bitters - 2 dashes</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the whisky, vermouth and bitters into a shaker woth ice.</li>
  <li>Shake until ice cold and serve into a martini glass.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a skewered cherry.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Other vermouths can work in this drink, but a sweet vermouth is preferred.</li>
    <li>The maraschino cherry is the best for this cocktail.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

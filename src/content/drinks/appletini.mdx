  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> USA</p>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Vodka - 50ml</li>
  <li>Apple juice - 50ml</li>
  <li>Lemon juice - 15ml</li>
  <li>Simple syrup - 15ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Cherry - 1</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Combine the syrup, vodka, apple juice and lemon juice with ice in a shaker.</li>
  <li>Shake until ice cold.</li>
  <li>Strain into a martini glass.</li>
  <li>Use a skewered cherry to garnish.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>A marachino cherry is preferred.</li>
    <li>You can add apple schnapps or apple syrup to the drink to give it a stronger taste.</li>
    <li>To make for a deeper taste, you can add a combination of both vodka and brandy, Calvados would be preferred - it is a French brandy made from apples.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

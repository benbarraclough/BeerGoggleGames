<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A spiced long drink with deep flavours and sweet accents, something simple but not easily found at a bar.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Collins glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Trinidad & Tobago</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Spiced rum - 50ml</li>
  <li>Simple syrup - 15ml</li>
  <li>Angostura bitters - 10ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 25ml</li>
  <li>Mint - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Start by adding the mint leaves and simple syrup into a Collins glass and gently muddle/stir.</li>
  <li>Add the rum, lime juice, and 2 dashes of bitters.</li>
  <li>Fill the glass with ice and stir with a bar spoon until the glass is cold and frosted.</li>
  <li>Add a little more ice bringing it to the top of the glass.</li>
  <li>Finish with the a few dashes of bitters, a little syrup and a sprig of mint.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Use demarara rum to recreate the original recipe.</li>
    <li>Crushed ice is preferable for this long drink.</li>
    <li>Demerara syrup is prefered to contribute to the deeper spiced taste of the drink.</li>
    <li>Keep the mint at the bottom of the drink to create the colour transition.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

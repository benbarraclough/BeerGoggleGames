<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A classic sweet and sour drink with a predominant taste of lemon and a frothy top.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Rocks glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> Egg</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Cyprus</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Brandy - 60ml</li>
  <li>Lemon juice -</li>
  <li>Cherry syrup - 15ml</li>
  <li>Angostura bitters - 3 dashes</li>
  <li>Egg white - 1/2</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Cherry - 1</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the brandy, syrup, bitters, lemon juice and egg white into a shaker with ice.</li>
  <li>Shake until the shaker is ice cold.</li>
  <li>Strain into a glass and garnish with a cherry and lemon on a skewer.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Freshly squeezed lemon juice is always best for this drink, as this is the main flavour of the drink.</li>
    <li>A maraschino cherry is the preferred choice of cherry.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

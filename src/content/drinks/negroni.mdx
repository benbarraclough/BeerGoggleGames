<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A bitter cocktail served neat with a citrusy yet sharp taste, you will either love it or hate it.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Rocks glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Italy</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Gin - 25ml</li>
  <li>Vermouth - 25ml</li>
  <li>Campari - 25ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 Handful</li>
  <li>Orange - 1 Slice</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the gin, vermouth and Campari into a shaker with some ice and shake well until the outside of the shaker feels cold.</li>
  <li>Strain the contents into a glass with ice</li>
  <li>Garnish with a slice of orange and serve</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Add simple syrup or a flavoured citrus gin to make the drink less bitter and more sweet</li>
    <li>Use one large ice cube in the glass to make the drink complete</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

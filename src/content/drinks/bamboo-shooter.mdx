<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Sherry - 30ml</li>
  <li>Bianco vermouth - 30ml</li>
  <li>Angostura bitters - 2 dashes</li>
  <li>Orange bitters - 2 dashes</li>
  <li>Lemon juice - 2 dashes</li>
  <li>Salt - 1 pinch</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the sherry, vermouth, lemon juice and bitters into a shaker with ice.</li>
  <li>Shake until the shaker turns ice cold.</li>
  <li>Strain into a shot glass and garnish with a sprinkle of salt</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Dry or bianco vrmouth should be used, not sweet or rosso.</li>
    <li>Fino or manzanilla sherry should be used for this drink.</li>
    <li>The oils of lemon and lime rind can be used in addition to the ingredients.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

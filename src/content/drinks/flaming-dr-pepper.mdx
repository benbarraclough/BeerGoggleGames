<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> The fiery mix of sweet Amaretto, bubbly beer and overproof rum simulate the iconic soft drink remarkably well.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 4 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Pint and shot glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> Almond nuts</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Mexico</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Amaretto - 35ml</li>
  <li>Overproof rum - 15ml</li>
  <li>Lager - 1/2 pint</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Pour the Amaretto in the bottom of the shot glass</li>
  <li>Top this shot off slowly with overproof rum, making sure it sits on top of the Amaretto</li>
  <li>Pour the lager in the pint glass</li>
  <li>Light the shot on fire with a match or lighter</li>
  <li>Drop the shot glass in the pint and you're good to go</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Don't fill the shot glass right to the top in case of spillage when picking it up, the overproof rum is will stay lit until extinguished.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

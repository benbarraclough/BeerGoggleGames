<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Vodka - 50ml</li>
  <li>Cranberry juice - 100ml</li>
  <li>Grapefruit juice - 50ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Lime - 1</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the vodka and juices to a shaker with ice.</li>
  <li>Shake until ice cold, then pour into a glass.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a slice of lime.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>The fresher the juice the better, use real fruit for a better quality drink.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

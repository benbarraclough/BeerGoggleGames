<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Vanilla vodka - 25ml</li>
  <li>Passion fruit liqueur - 15ml</li>
  <li>Passion fruit juice - 25ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 10ml</li>
  <li>Passion fruit - 1 half</li>
  <li>Prosecco (served separately) - 1 shot glass</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add the ice, vodka, liqueur and juices to a shaker.</li>
  <li>Shake vigorously until chilled.</li>
  <li>Pour whilst straining into a martini glass.</li>
  <li>Place half a passion fruit on top as garnish.</li>
  <li>Serve with a shot of prosecco to pour into the cocktail or drink separately.</li>
  <li>Passoa is used as the liqueur for the best pornstar martinis.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A sweet and sour cocktail with a rich taste and nutty essence.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Rocks glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> Egg & nuts</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Italy</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Amaretto - 50ml</li>
  <li>Lemon juice - 25ml</li>
  <li>Egg - 1</li>
  <li>Cherry syrup - 10ml</li>
  <li>Cherry - 1</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Put the amaretto, lemon juice, syrup and a tablespoon of egg white into a mixer and shake.</li>
  <li>Add ice and shake again until well chilled.</li>
  <li>Pour into a glass with ice.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a cherry to finish.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Use angostura syrup instead of cherry syrup for a slightly different taste.</li>
    <li>Use brandied cherries for an added element to the drink. Skewer and garnish the drink with them.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

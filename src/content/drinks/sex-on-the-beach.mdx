<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A fruity cocktail that goes down very easily, combining peach, cranberry and orange.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Highball glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> None</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Florida, USA</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Vodka - 50ml</li>
  <li>Peach Schnapps - 25ml</li>
  <li>Cranberry juice - 50ml</li>
  <li>Orange juice - 50ml</li>
  <li>Orange - 1 Slice</li>
  <li>Ice</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Fill a glass with ice</li>
  <li>Put ice, vodka and peach schnapps and the juices to a shaker</li>
  <li>Shake until very cold and strain into the glass</li>
  <li>Add a slice of orange to the glass as garnish</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Add Chambord as an additional ingredient to spice up the drink.</li>
    <li>Gently pour the juices in one after the other to create a colour gradient in the drink.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

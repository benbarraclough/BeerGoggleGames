  <p><strong>Description:</strong> A citrus infused vodka cocktail with a sweet and sharp taste, served in a martini glass.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/images/liquor.webp">
  <ul>
  <li>Lemon vodka - 50ml</li>
  <li>Triple sec - 15ml</li>
  <li>Cranberry juice - 30ml</li>
  <li>Lime juice - 10ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Orange (to garnish) - 1</li>
</ul>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add ice, 3 parts vodka, 2 parts cranberry juice, 1 part triple sec & 1 part lime juice to a cocktail shaker.</li>
  <li>Shake until the shaker is ice cold.</li>
  <li>Strain the drink into a martini glass.</li>
  <li>Garnish with a slice of orange peel.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>To make a twisted orange garnish, twist and heat one edge with a flame.</li>
    <li>If you don't have lemon vodka, use regular vodka and lemon liqueur or lemon juice.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

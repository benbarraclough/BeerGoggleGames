<DrinkSection title="Overview" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/info.webp">
  <p><strong>Description:</strong> This popular beverage combines just 3 ingredients, providing a smooth and pleasant coffee flavour as seen in The Big Lebowski.</p>
  <p><strong>Time:</strong> 5 mins</p>
  <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium</p>
  <p><strong>Glass type:</strong> Rocks glass</p>
  <p><strong>Dietary Notes:</strong> Dairy (not vegan)</p>
  <p><strong>Origin:</strong> Russia</p>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Vodka - 50ml</li>
  <li>Coffee Liqueur - 25ml</li>
  <li>Cream - 15ml</li>
  <li>Ice - 1 handful</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Add ice to a rocks glass along with a shot of vodka and half a shot of coffee liqueur.</li>
  <li>Mix these together with a stirer or give it a quick swirl.</li>
  <li>Slowly pour the cream in and you're ready to serve.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Milk can be used in place of cream, however a heavy cream will give a richer taste.</li>
    <li>Slowly pour the cream in using a spoon to delicately layer the liquid on top of what is already in the glass to create a colour gradient.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>

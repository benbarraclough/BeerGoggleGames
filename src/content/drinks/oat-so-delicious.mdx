<DrinkSection title="Ingredients" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/liquor.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Rum - 60ml</li>
  <li>Oat milk - 100ml</li>
  <li>Simple syrup - 15ml</li>
  <li>Cream - 1 Tbsp</li>
  <li>Cinnamon - 1/2 Tsp</li>
  <li>Nutmeg - 1/2 Tsp</li>
  <li>Popcorn - 1 handful</li>
  <li>Ice - 2 handfuls</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Recipe" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/rules.webp">
  <ol>
  <li>Spoon the nutmeg and cinnamon into the bottom of the shaker, then pour in the simple syrup and muddle so that the spices combine and dissolve.</li>
  <li>Add the rum, oat milk, cream and 1 handful of ice then shake until ice cold.</li>
  <li>Strain the contents into the glass over fresh ice.</li>
  <li>Top with a handful of popcorn and serve with a straw.</li>
</ol>
</DrinkSection>

<DrinkSection title="Tips" icon="/BeerGoggleGames/images/lightbulb.webp">
  <ul>
    <li>Regular milk can be used, for a thicker drink use a full fat milk.</li>
    <li>Caramel is a good additional flavour, using either syrup, sauce or toffee popcorn.</li>
  </ul>
</DrinkSection>
